About Me

For a long time, I thought I was the refined type that listens to classical music, while painting a scenery sitting on the balcony, and sipping wine. Who also, enjoys dressing up and fixing their make-up and hair every day.

Turns out I am a hint of the above with the combination of the dirty ole tom-boy who likes to dig in the dirt, ride four wheelers, enjoys camping, fishing, hiking, spending the day on the lake and listens to load country, rock, and pop music. Like “Hail to the King”, when I really want to jam it out. My hair is up or down and only seeing a curling iron, maybe 5 times a year. My make-up style is bare, with a splash of mascara and tinted ChapStick. I enjoy volunteering at my kids’ school when I can and only embarrass my children once in a while. Arts and crafts… meh, sometimes.

I am a wife of almost 7 years, a mother to one daughter, 12, and two step-sons, 11 and 15. Every day I try to push myself to be better than I was the day before and I work really hard to display an example of a good work ethic and a positive attitude.
